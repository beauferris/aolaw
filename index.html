Ali Omar, B.A, J.D. Barrister and Solicitor

Since my admission to the Law Society of Alberta,

I have successfully represented clients deal with a vast array of legal problems from civil disputes to criminal indictments. No matter what your issue, I will work tirelessly to give you your best chance at solving it.
