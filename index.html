Ali is a new lawyer who is also new to Red Deer. Born in Calgary, Ali completed a Bachelor`s of Arts in Economics at the University of Calgary in 2016. Ali received an full-ride scholarship to attend law school at Georgia State University-College of Law in Atlanta, Georgia. He spent 4 years in Atlanta where he fought to help Defendants in the overburdened and overbearing American criminal justice system.

As an intern with the Georgia State Public Defenders Office, Ali worked in the Complex Felony division. He gained experience defending persons accused of serious felonies, including drug trafficking and murder. Even some cases that received national attention. His greatest contribution, and proudest moment as a lawyer, came when he drafted the legal argument that eventually led to the Georgia Court of Appeals decision expanding constitutional protections against unreasonable searches. He continued to volunteer with the Public Defender`s Office after graduation.

Ali graduated law school in January 2020 and returned home to Alberta. In August 2020, he successfully completed the required exams for internationally trained lawyers. Ali joined Vesta Law as an articling student in May 2021, and has been fighting for clients across central Alberta. As a lawyer he has successfully helped clients with issues in the realm of family law, criminal law, civil litigation, real estate and wills/estates.

In his free time Ali enjoys, playing tennis, collecting Pokemon Cards and being overly critical of movies.
