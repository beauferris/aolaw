Ali Omar, B.A, J.D. Barrister and Solicitor

I became a lawyer because I enjoy feeling useful to others. When I take on a client I see it as my job to help them navigate the legal system successfully to reach their goals. I am here to shoulder the burden that is a bit to heavy and overwhelming for the average untrained civilian. In 2016 I received a scholarship to study law at Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia.

I got to learn under some of the most accomplished criminal lawyers in the U.S.A. I got the opportunity to cut my teeth with Atlanta Public Defenders Office on the Complex Felony Unit. I got experience working on drug trafficking, illegal searches, and serious violent crimes. I returned to Alberta and began my Canadian Legal Career in 2021. Since my admission to the Law Society of Alberta,

I have successfully represented clients deal with a vast array of legal problems from civil disputes to criminal indictments. No matter what your issue, I will work tirelessly to give you your best chance at solving it.
